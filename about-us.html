Our Value-Added Approach to Outsourcing

The traditional outsourcing model just doesn’t cut it. Here’s how TopBoffin is doing it better:

Tailored to the UK market

We’re not a catch-all outsourcing solution – TopBoffin specifically caters to businesses in the UK. That means we’re fully GDPR-compliant and follow all the UK’s regulations, we understand how UK businesses operate, and our Boffins understand the culture.

More than just filling roles

Forget what you’ve heard about outsourcing because we’re not here to check skills off a list or bombard you with CVs. Businesses in the UK choose TopBoffin because we connect them with dedicated, exceptional talent, committed to growing with you.

Managed Infrastructure

Our Multi-Stage Screening Process

We customise our screening process to find your ideal hire. Here’s a quick overview of how our screening usually goes.

Initial screening

Our initial round of screening involves vetting CVs and calling applicants up to learn more about their qualifications. Applicants that stand out move along to the next screening stage.

Cultural fit

Possessing the right skills and experience is crucial, but Boffins also excel at integrating with your work culture. That’s why we screen promising applicants for cultural fit.

Skills assessment

CVs and interviews only reveal so much, so we work with you to design custom, role-specific tests to make sure applicants can deliver what you need.
