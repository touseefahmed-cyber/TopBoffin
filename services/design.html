Why Sourcing Design Talent in the UK is Challenging

Competition for top talent

The UK’s web and graphic design industries are growing, and the competition for top design talent is fierce. Smaller businesses are left with slimmer pickings.

Retention issues

The attrition rates for designers are high – and when a designer leaves, companies struggle with gaps in productivity and delays in project timelines.

The cost

According to Payscale, the average salary for a graphic designer in the UK is £23911 annually, and it’s as high as £30226 for a web designer. Salary levels for experienced designers can be much higher.
