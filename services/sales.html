Why Traditional Sales Outsourcing is Flawed

Linguistic shortcomings

Customers in the UK expect to hear from people that sound like them. Outsourcing your sales functions to teams with weak language skills can hurt your close rates and your brand image.

Scripts over pitches

Top sales professionals need to understand your customers' pain points and respond to them with solutions – they need to make your pitch in the best way. But traditional sales outsourcing involves scripted pitches that your customers see right through.

The empathy gap

In traditional outsourcing, sales reps aren’t in close contact with your in-house team. But to sell your product or services well, they need to understand and believe in what they’re selling.
