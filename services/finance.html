Why Hiring Finance Talent in the UK is Challenging

Skills shortages

The UK is facing a skills shortage in finance and accounts, and employers are struggling to meet their needs with their existing resources.

Challenges in retaining talent

Finance and accounts are essential business functions, but many employers in the UK are struggling to retain good finance professionals – leading to operational bottlenecks.

The cost

According to talent.com, the average finance salary in the UK is £41,386 annually, and more experienced professionals command up to £65,000. After factoring in recruiting and onboarding costs, you’re looking at paying even more during the first year.
